# Comilus Operating Boundaries

Comilus is a low-risk Shenzhen local coordination service. The business priority is survival first: only accept work that can be scoped, quoted, delivered, and documented without taking medical, legal, financial, insurance, immigration, or emergency responsibility.

## Accepted Work

- Routine checkup logistics
- Non-emergency appointment request communication
- Chinese-English practical interpretation
- Scheduled companion support
- Arrival, hotel, route, SIM, and city setup guidance
- Business visit logistics
- Meeting point and transport coordination
- Public contact and location lookup

## Hard Red Lines

Decline or redirect requests involving:

- Medical diagnosis
- Treatment recommendations
- Prescription help
- Test/result interpretation
- Emergency symptoms or urgent distress
- Insurance claims or reimbursement advice
- Visa, immigration, legal, tax, or investment advice
- Handling customer passwords, bank apps, QR payment authorization, cards, or payment credentials
- Holding provider fees without a written payment process
- Claims of provider partnership, priority access, guaranteed appointment, guaranteed English staff, guaranteed price, report timing, or outcome

## Medical-Adjacent Scope

Allowed wording:

- "routine checkup logistics"
- "appointment request communication"
- "arrival instructions"
- "practical interpretation"
- "companion support"
- "provider fees are separate"

Avoid wording:

- "medical consultation"
- "medical tourism"
- "best hospital"
- "priority access"
- "guaranteed appointment"
- "treatment plan"
- "we help you get treated"
- "we explain your diagnosis"

## Lead Triage

For every lead:

1. Confirm the request is non-emergency.
2. Confirm it is practical coordination, not professional advice.
3. Confirm date, location, language need, number of people, and expected time window.
4. Confirm whether an interpreter/companion is available before taking payment.
5. Send written scope, fee, exclusions, cancellation terms, and payment method.
6. Collect payment only after scope is accepted.

## Standard Refusal Script

```text
Thank you for the message. This request is outside Comilus scope because we provide local coordination and translation support only. We cannot provide medical, legal, insurance, financial, immigration, or emergency services. Please contact the appropriate licensed professional or local emergency service.
```

## Emergency Script

```text
Comilus cannot handle urgent or emergency situations. Please call local emergency services or go directly to an emergency department. We cannot provide medical triage, diagnosis, or emergency coordination.
```

## Contractor Rules

Every contractor must agree:

- No medical advice.
- No diagnosis or result interpretation.
- No provider promises.
- No handling customer payment credentials.
- No side deals with Comilus customers.
- No storing customer documents.
- Report incidents immediately.
- Keep customer information confidential.

## Payment Rules

- Readiness review and coordination: 100% prepaid.
- Companion service: 50% deposit to reserve; balance due 24 hours before start.
- Provider fees, transport, meals, parking, tickets, and third-party costs are separate.
- Third-party costs are refundable only if the third party refunds them.
- If Comilus cannot deliver an accepted paid service, refund the undelivered Comilus fee.

## Survival Rule

When uncertain, decline the work or narrow the scope. A missed order is cheaper than a legal, medical, payment, or reputation problem.
